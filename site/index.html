Guide to Ratings

99   Top Shelf Rating (98-100)

This is as good as it gets. Books with this badge have to earn it! This one is worth a reread.


92   High Shelf (90-97)

Think of this as your personal stamp of approval. You shouldn't think twice about recommending a document with this badge to a family member or a friend.


87   Mid Shelf (80-89)

This was worth your time to read and may have been very strong in one aspect or another, but you probably won't be grabbing it off the shelf except to reference a passage.


75   Low Shelf (70-79)

This isn't a write off. Maybe it had a compelling chapter or a great side-character. But you might not choose to read it if you had to do it all over again.


43   Bottom Shelf (0-69)

Let's never talk about this book again. This badge is applied only as a warning so you don't recommend it.
